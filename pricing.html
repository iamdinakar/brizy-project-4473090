Is choosing us a good choice?

Few questions you might have — answered

Do you have a free plan?

No, we don't have a free plan.

However, we do have a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. To protect ourselves from spam and abuse, some features are limited on the free trial.

Which plan is best for me? Help me choose

Solo — as the name suggests, is meant for solo entrepreneurs and founders. It is carefully structured to help you if you're starting out.

Startup — is meant for startup companies and small to medium sales teams. We help up to 15 team members on this plan.

Growth — helps companies in growth hacking stage and scaleups. You can onboard up to unlimited team members and work with your entire sales team. All our features are unlimited on this plan. We wish you growth.

What if I go over limits?

You needn't worry, most of our customers never go over the limits. In case you did, you can upgrade to the next plan in a single click.

Can I cancel, upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes, you can cancel, upgrade, downgrade or pause your subscription anytime. Just a single button click.

Do you have any discounts for non-profits or early startups?

We work hard and smart in keeping our product as affordable and accessible as possible. Our current pricing model is the best that we can offer for our customers. Subscribe and reach out to our support, so that if we have a better pricing or discount in the future, we'll let you know.
